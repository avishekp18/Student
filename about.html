Our Funky History

Funky Student Management System was founded in 2020 with the aim of simplifying the educational process by creating a centralized platform for managing student data.

Over the years, we have grown into a leading provider of educational software, serving institutions worldwide with cutting-edge technology and exceptional customer support.
